Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes

Find more information here:


Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes were a seven member band from the planet Clak'dor VII. They were best known for their jazzy style of Jizz and Jatz. At the height of their career the Nodes enjoyed popularity across the galaxy. However after some questionable business decisions and a general discontent among the band members, the bookings grew sparse and the Nodes found themselves performing mostly in smoky dank cantinas.
